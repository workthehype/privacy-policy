Location Attendance ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location data (including background location)
  • User identification details (such as user ID or employee ID)
  • Attendance timestamps (Punch In / Punch Out)
  • Device-related information for security and fraud prevention

Location Data Usage

Location Attendance collects location data, including background location, to enable attendance tracking, geo-fencing validation, and workplace presence verification.

Location data may be collected even when the app is closed or not in use, but only after the user provides explicit consent through a prominent in-app disclosure.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Attendance management and verification
  • Preventing fake or proxy attendance
  • Generating attendance reports for authorized administrators

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

User Control and Permissions

  • Location permissions are requested only after showing a prominent disclosure
  • Users may grant or revoke location access at any time via device settings
  • Disabling location access may limit attendance-related features
